What is Zero to One?

0 ➝ 1: True Innovation
1 ➝ n: Copying & Scaling

“Every moment in business happens only once.”

Author: Peter Thiel with Blake Masters

Published: 2014

Overview: 'Zero to One' offers bold insights into the startup world. Thiel encourages entrepreneurs to build groundbreaking businesses, rather than just improving what exists.

Main Premise: Going from 0 to 1 means doing something completely new—creating rather than competing.

Why Read This Book? It changes how you think about innovation, entrepreneurship, and competition. It provides mental models for identifying secrets, creating monopolies, and pursuing bold goals.
